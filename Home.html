Welcome to Yokai Senjafuda

Digital Collection of Japanese ghosts and monsters

This digital exhibition focuses on tiny slips of paper senjafuda TittL that depict japanese ghosts and monsters -yökai Both senjafuda and yökai have their roots in Japanese popular culture in the early modern period (17th- 19th centuries), and both continue to cast a spell on viewers today.

The phenomenon of senjafuda dates back to the late 18th century. They were originally made by pilgrims to paste on the walls of temples and shrines as a sort of devotional graffiti. Later they became collector's items…
